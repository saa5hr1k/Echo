◎ ECHO — Your AI Body Double
=================================

WHAT IT IS
Echo is an AI companion that stays with you while you study, work, and chase
goals. It turns real progress into an RPG: focus sessions earn XP, you level
up, build 4 skills (Knowledge, Discipline, Fitness, Creativity), keep streaks,
and complete daily + weekly quests. A friendly AI companion reacts to your wins
and a Goal Coach gives you practical plans.

------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO RUN IT (two ways)
------------------------------------------------------------------

EASIEST — just double-click "index.html"
  It opens in your browser and everything works: companion, focus sessions,
  XP, levels, quests, streaks, and the smart built-in Goal Coach.

FULL VERSION — double-click "Start-Echo.bat"  (needs Node.js installed)
  This runs a tiny local server at http://localhost:8124. You only need this
  if you want to plug in a real AI brain (see below). It opens automatically.

------------------------------------------------------------------
OPTIONAL: GIVE THE COMPANION A REAL AI BRAIN
------------------------------------------------------------------
Out of the box the companion uses smart built-in responses — no setup, works
offline. To make conversations truly intelligent:

  1. Get a FREE Google Gemini key at  https://aistudio.google.com/apikey
  2. Run Echo with "Start-Echo.bat" (the real AI needs the local server)
  3. Click the ⚙️ Settings gear (top right) and paste your key, then Save.

Your key is stored only on your own computer.

------------------------------------------------------------------
YOUR DATA
------------------------------------------------------------------
Everything (profile, XP, streaks, goals) is saved in your browser on this
device. Use the same browser to keep your progress. Reset anytime in Settings.

Have fun leveling up real life. 🚀
